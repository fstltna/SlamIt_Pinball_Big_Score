MINI MODS OVERVIEW

















BANK

SHOOT GLOWING LOOP AND

GLOWING RAMP TO ACTIVATE.

GOAL IS TO INSERT THE BALL

INTO THE BANK.

JAIL

JAIL IS ACTIVATED IF YOU INSERT

BALL INTO BANK AND NOT ALL

BANK TARGETS ARE DOWN.

SHOOT AT U-TURN TO LOCK THE

BALL IN THE JAIL.

WITH A NEW BALL YOU SHOOT

THE BALL THAT IS IN THE JAIL FOR

ESCAPE. AFTER THAT YOU

PLAY TWO-BALL


IN-OUT

AFTER A SNEAK-IN, YOU MUST

SHOOT AT START MODE TO

ACTIVATE IN-OUT MINI MOD.

SMALL RAMP IS UP AND THE

SAFE IS OPENED. THROW THE BALL

INTO THE SAFE TO COMPLETE

IN-OUT.


MONEY

MONEY IS ACTIVATED ON SHOOTING

TWO GLOWING RAMPS.

SHOOT AT MOENY TARGTES NEXT

TO RIGHT RAMP.

LEFT MOENY TARGET GIVES YOU

MONEY, FORTUNE, FAME. AFTER

THAT SHOOT THE RIGHT RAMP FOR

RASING LEVEL OF MONEY THAT

YOU ALWAYS GET ON HITING THE

RIGHT MONEY TARGET.


CHASE

SHOOT GLOWING LOOP THREE TIMES

IN A ROW TO ACTIVATE CHASE.

BRIDGE IS UP AND YOU NEED TO

MAKE TWO BRIDGE LOOPS IN A ROW

TO COMPLETE THE CHASE.


CALLS

CALLS ARE ACTIVATED

AUTOMATICALY IF THERE WAS

NO REAL HIT FOR 20 SECONDS.

FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS OF

A CALL TO COMPLETE 8 POSSIBLE

TASKS.


BOXING

HIT LOOP AND RAMP TARGETS

THAT SHOW UP.
